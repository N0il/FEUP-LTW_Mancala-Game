Rules

  • Each player controls their side's cavities and the storage to their right.
  • Each player takes a turn choosing one of their cavities, that cavity's seeds are then distributed one by one into the following cavities/storages (excluding the opposite player's storage).
  • If the last seed you place lands in your own storage, you can play again, if it lands in an empty cavity in your own side, you take all the seeds on the opposing side's cavity into your cavity.
  • The game ends when one player can't play due to not having anymore seeds in their cavities. If the other player still has seeds, he can transfer them to his storage.
  • At the end of the game, each player counts the number of seeds they have in their storage, the winner is the one with the most seeds.
